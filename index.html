Guess The Number?

How to play!?

  • Click on Play now to start playing
  • Use the dialog boxes that appear to play the game
  • Specify your own range of numbers to guess a number from
  • Guess the random number in 5 attempts or less
  • Use the hints to know if you are guessing higher or lower than the actual number
  • Click play again after a game to restart the game
